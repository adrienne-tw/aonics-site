Building autonomy for the world’s solar infrastructure

Aonics brings together robotics, computer vision, and operations software so utility-scale solar teams can inspect, respond, and plan with confidence—on the ground and from the control room.

How we got here

  • Built in the field

    We focused first on what crews actually do on site—walking rows, logging issues, coordinating follow-up—so the product stays grounded in real O&M workflows.

  • Seeing the whole plant

    Computer vision and robotics are pieces of a larger picture: consistent capture, structured findings, and software that turns imagery into decisions operators trust.

  • Control room to crew

    We design for both sides of the fence—dispatchers planning work and technicians closing it out—so priorities stay aligned when conditions change mid-week.

  • Scaling with partners

    As portfolios grow, teams need tools that keep pace—repeatable routes, comparable data across sites, and room to adopt autonomy without retraining everyone from scratch.
